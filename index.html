Description

This poetry book, "The cosmic 8" is the debut book of Shivam Kumar. This is a short poetry book consisting of 8 poems. There is a total of 7 sonnets and one 12-line poem in Rondeau Prime form. The name of 7 (Cosmic) sonnets and the topic they consist are as follow:- 1) Fierce Circle of life- Philosophical journey of life from beginning to end. 2) The Curse, The Penurious- A tough Vocabulary, critical poetry on "Poverty". 3 Open to the Garland's Bed- Emotions of this poem cannot be written in 1-2 lines! 4) O dear! My Love- This is a Romantic sonnet showing the feelings of a person for his loved one. This poetry consists of a total of 42-Rhetorical devices, the highest number of Rhetorical devices used in poetry& Sonnet ever! 5) I Swear to You, to God- A Sonnetic confession of Love! 6) Laughing- This poem gives you deep insights into how the world has forgotten to Laugh. 7) The Tale of a boy and Angel- This is a poem about a boy who surprisingly gets into a very confusing situation and he has to save an angel there! This is the Shortest Fantasy poem with ultimate rhyming. There is one 12-line poem written using the basic framework of the Rondeau Prime form of poetry. Gap- Light on the vast difference between poor and rich and their generations. This is poetry with the highest number of Anaphora used in a 12-line poem.
